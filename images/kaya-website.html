Our Philosophy

Jewellery has always
been about the
occasion.
We think it's about
the person.

KAYA — from Sanskrit, meaning body, the self — was born in Delhi with one question: why does jewellery only come out for weddings and festivals? The most intimate thing you can wear deserves to be worn every day, for yourself alone.

Every piece we make starts not with a stone or a design, but with a woman. Her posture. Her skin. The way she moves through a room. We design jewellery that disappears into who you are — and makes you more of it.
